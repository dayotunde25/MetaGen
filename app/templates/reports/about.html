Dataset Health Reports

What are Dataset Health Reports?

Dataset Health Reports are comprehensive assessments of your dataset's quality and compliance with metadata standards. These reports provide detailed insights into various quality dimensions and offer recommendations for improvement.

The reports are designed to help you:

  • Understand the overall quality of your datasets
  • Identify specific areas for improvement
  • Track compliance with FAIR principles and Schema.org standards
  • Share dataset quality information with your team or stakeholders
  • Document dataset health for research and governance purposes

Report Contents

Each health report includes the following sections:

Dataset Information

Basic information about your dataset, including title, description, source, format, and other relevant metadata.

Quality Metrics

Detailed quality scores across multiple dimensions:

  • Completeness: Presence of required and recommended metadata fields
  • Consistency: Consistency of data formats and values
  • Accuracy: Accuracy and correctness of the data content
  • Timeliness: Freshness and update frequency of the dataset
  • Conformity: Compliance with standards like Schema.org and FAIR principles
  • Integrity: Data integrity and validation
FAIR Compliance

Assessment of compliance with FAIR principles:

  • Findable: Data can be found by humans and machines
  • Accessible: Data can be accessed with appropriate authentication
  • Interoperable: Data can be integrated with other data and used by applications
  • Reusable: Data can be reused under clear licensing and provenance
Recommendations

Actionable recommendations for improving your dataset's quality and compliance.

Issues

Identified issues that need to be addressed to improve your dataset's quality.

How to Generate a Health Report

You can generate a health report for any dataset that has undergone quality assessment:

  1. Navigate to a dataset's detail page or quality assessment page
  2. Look for the "Health Report" button in the actions area
  3. Choose between HTML (for viewing in browser) or PDF (for download) format
  4. View or download your report
Note: Health reports are only available for datasets that have completed quality assessment.

Sharing Reports

Once generated, health reports can be:

  • Saved for your records
  • Shared with colleagues via email or other collaboration tools
  • Included in research documentation or data management plans
  • Regenerated at any time to reflect the latest quality assessment
